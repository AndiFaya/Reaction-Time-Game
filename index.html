How to Play

  1. Enter your name and choose your settings on the page below.
  2. Click Start Game to open the game window.
  3. In the game window, click Start Test to begin round 1.
  4. The signal area will show WAIT, do NOT react yet.
  5. When it turns GO!react immediately.
  6. If your mode is Click, click the signal area with your MOUSE.
    If it is Spacebar, press the spacebar KEY.
    If it is Mixed,check the INSTRUCTION BAR above the signal area each round.
  7. Your reaction time appears after each round. The next round starts automatically.
